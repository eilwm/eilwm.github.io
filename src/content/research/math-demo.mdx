Inline math like $e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$ should render.

Block math:

$$
\nabla \cdot \mathbf{u} = 0
$$

and

$$
\frac{\partial \mathbf{u}}{\partial t}
+ (\mathbf{u}\cdot\nabla)\mathbf{u}
= -\nabla p + \nu \nabla^2 \mathbf{u}
$$
